## Overview

aquazen is a dynamic platform that bridges the gap between independent professionals and clients. Whether you are a freelancer looking for your next big project or a business searching for specialized skills, our app provides a secure and efficient way to connect, collaborate, and get work done.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Mobile Monitoring**: Track essential water quality parameters, specifically pH levels and water volume, instantly through a user-friendly smartphone application.

- **Automated Environment Control**: Automatically regulate the pond's ecosystem, including adjusting pH levels and managing water volume to prevent overflow during heavy rainfall.

- **Smart Alert System**: Receive instant automated notifications whenever water parameters fall outside optimal safety thresholds (e.g., extreme acidity/alkalinity or abnormal water levels).

- **Historical Data Tracking**: Access a comprehensive log of water quality changes over time, allowing for better analysis and long-term pond management.

Versatile Pond Compatibility: Engineered for flexibility, the system is fully compatible with various fish farming setups, including advanced biofloc technology.
## Technologies Used

- **Flutter**: Utilized as the primary framework for developing the cross-platform mobile application. It allowed for the creation of a highly responsive, intuitive, and visually appealing user interface that enables fish farmers to easily monitor real-time pond conditions directly from their smartphones.

- **Dart**: The core programming language used in conjunction with Flutter to construct the application's robust frontend logic. Its efficient asynchronous programming capabilities ensured smooth data processing and seamless real-time UI updates without interrupting the user experience.

- **Firebase**: Implemented as a comprehensive backend-as-a-service (BaaS) solution. It powers the real-time database to synchronize sensor data instantly across the mobile app, while also handling the automated push notification system to immediately alert users of any critical water quality changes.

- **Arduino**: Served as the central microcontroller and hardware brain of the IoT ecosystem. It was programmed to continuously interface with the physical water quality sensors (such as pH and water volume sensors), process the raw environmental data, and reliably transmit this crucial information to the database.

## Outcome

The implementation of AquaZen significantly enhances fish farming efficiency by minimizing manual water monitoring. By providing real-time data access, automated environmental controls, and instant hazard alerts, the system effectively mitigates the risk of delayed responses to water quality fluctuations. This project successfully demonstrates the power of IoT integration in maintaining a healthy, optimized aquatic environment and boosting overall aquaculture productivity.
